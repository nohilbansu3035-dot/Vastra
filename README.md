# 🎨 Sagar Bansu - Textile Design Portfolio

Hello there! I'm **Sagar Bansu**, a professional textile designer with over 5 years of practical industry experience. 

My work revolves around understanding client requirements, market trends, and translating them into unique, commercially viable textile patterns. I specialize in design development, repeat pattern creation, and screen printing design, ensuring every artwork is ready for real-world application.

## 🌟 Core Competencies

- **Design Development:** Turning reference concepts into highly refined, original artworks.
- **Repeat Systems:** Expertise in half drop, mirror, block, and placement patterns.
- **Screen Printing Setup:** In-depth knowledge of print processes, color separations, and technical limitations.
- **Color Balancing:** Building effective color stories that resonate with current market trends.
- **Fabric Knowledge:** Understanding how different designs behave across a variety of textiles.

## 📂 Portfolio Categories

Within this portfolio, you will find curated galleries showcasing my specializations:

- **Saree Design:** Specialized Saree pattern designs built for domestic and international production.
- **Front Back Design:** Structured curation of front and back apparel graphics.
- **Repeat Pattern:** Seamless repeat patterns built for effortless scaling.
- **Allover Design:** Expansive allover aesthetics ready for scalable fabric printing.
- **Pattern:** Specific individual patterns exploring color balancing and texture.

## 💼 Experience & Approach

Over the past 5+ years, I’ve learned that a design is only successful if it works in production and sells in the market. This understanding shapes every project I take on. I don’t just design for aesthetics; I design with production, printing techniques, and end-use in mind.
